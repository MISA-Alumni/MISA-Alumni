About

Welcome to the home of the Management Information Systems Association Alumni Network.

Management Information Systems Association is a student-run academic organization serving California State University, Sacramento.

MISA hosts weekly meetings with IT professionals, inviting guest speakers from a variety of industries to talk about their work in the MIS (Management Information Systems) field.

Various consultants, programmers, and supervisors speak to MISA about their roles in the IT field. Many of these organizations they work for include the Big Four Accounting Firms, IT consultancies, cybersecurity consultancies, credit unions, government agencies, and energy corporations.

MISA's goal is to connect students with jobs, and many of these events host recruiters looking to hire students for internships and eventual full-time opportunities.


MISA Alumni Network

The goal of the MISA Alumni Network is to allow graduates to continue engaging with their former peers and mentors to build a dynamic network within their field of study.

We provide a platform for where members can meet once per year and catch up on current and past positions, latest trends in emerging technologies, and which companies are hiring.
